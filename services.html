Who We Are

LandCad Services is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra. Founded with a strong technical foundation, we serve government, infrastructure, corporate and private sector clients across the region.

Our work philosophy is built on accuracy, compliance and efficiency. We combine traditional surveying expertise with modern instruments, drone technology and GIS-based workflows to deliver dependable results for critical projects.
