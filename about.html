About LandCad Services

Who We Are

LandCad Services is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company delivering accurate, compliant and cost-efficient survey outputs for infrastructure, government and private developments.

We combine field expertise with modern technology including Total Station, DGPS, UAVs, GIS and CAD-based processing workflows.

Our focus remains on precision, timelines, regulatory compliance and long-term client trust.
