Who We Are

LandCad Services is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company based in Nagpur, Maharashtra. Established to deliver accuracy, reliability and technology-driven results, we support government, infrastructure, corporate and private sector projects.

Our expertise spans land surveying, engineering surveys, drone-based aerial mapping and GIS services. We combine modern equipment with experienced professionals to ensure every project meets regulatory standards and client expectations.

Our Vision

To become a trusted technology partner in land surveying and geospatial services by delivering accurate, innovative and sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To provide precise surveying and mapping services using advanced tools, skilled professionals and strict quality control for infrastructure and development projects.
