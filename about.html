About LandCad Services

LandCad Services is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company based in Nagpur, Maharashtra, delivering accurate, reliable and regulation-compliant survey outputs for government, infrastructure and private sector projects.

With strong field expertise and adoption of modern surveying technologies, we support projects related to roads, railways, irrigation, mining, industrial layouts and urban development.

Our Core Strength

We focus on precision, project timelines, cost efficiency and data accuracy. Our survey methodologies are aligned with statutory requirements and client-specific technical standards.

Instruments We Use

  • Total Station (High-precision angular & distance measurement)
  • DGPS / GNSS Systems
  • UAV / Drone Survey Systems
  • Auto Levels & Digital Levels

Software & Technology

  • AutoCAD & Civil 3D
  • GIS Platforms (QGIS / ArcGIS)
  • Drone Data Processing Software (DSM / DTM / Orthomosaic)
  • Survey Data Processing & Reporting Tools

Why LandCad

  • Experienced field team with infrastructure exposure
  • Combination of traditional surveying & modern geospatial technology
  • Accurate deliverables with clear documentation
  • Timely execution and cost-effective survey solutions
  • Trusted by government, contractors and private developers
