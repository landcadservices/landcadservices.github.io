About LandCad Services

Professional Land Surveying & Geospatial Solutions Partner

Company Overview

LandCad Services is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra. We specialize in delivering high-accuracy surveying, mapping and spatial data services for government, infrastructure, industrial and private sector projects.

Our operations are driven by precision, compliance and technology. By integrating advanced surveying instruments, drone technology and GIS platforms, we support planning, construction, monitoring and asset management across a wide range of infrastructure and development projects.

Survey Instruments & Technology

To ensure accuracy and reliability, LandCad Services deploys modern, calibrated surveying instruments operated by trained professionals.

  • Total Station (Engineering & Layout Surveys)
  • D-GPS / GNSS Receivers
  • UAV / Drone Survey Systems
  • Auto Levels & Digital Levels
  • Advanced Field Data Collection Tools

Software & Data Processing

We use industry-standard software for survey data processing, analysis and visualization, ensuring accurate and actionable deliverables.

  • AutoCAD & Civil 3D
  • ArcGIS / QGIS
  • Drone Data Processing Software (Orthomosaic, DSM / DTM)
  • Contour, Volume & Area Computation Tools
  • GIS Database & Thematic Mapping Platforms

Project Execution & Cost Efficiency

LandCad Services follows a structured project execution methodology focused on accuracy, time efficiency and cost optimization. Our workflows are designed to minimize rework, reduce site delays and ensure faster data availability.

This approach helps clients achieve better project control, reduced operational costs and dependable survey outcomes, even under challenging site conditions.

Commitment & Quality Assurance

Every project undertaken by LandCad Services is supported by strict quality checks, safety practices and compliance with technical standards. We are committed to delivering accurate, reliable and transparent survey results.

Our dedication to professionalism and long-term partnerships has enabled us to build trust with government bodies, infrastructure developers and private organizations.
