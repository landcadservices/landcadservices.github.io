About LandCad Services

LandCad Services is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra. We specialize in delivering high-accuracy surveying, mapping and spatial data services for government, infrastructure, industrial and private sector projects.

Our work is driven by precision, compliance and technology. By integrating advanced surveying instruments, drone technology and GIS platforms, we support critical planning, construction and asset management decisions across a wide range of projects.

Instruments & Technology

  • Total Station (High Precision Engineering Grade)
  • D-GPS / GNSS Receivers
  • UAV / Drone Survey Systems
  • Auto Levels & Digital Levels
  • Advanced Field Data Collection Tools

All instruments are calibrated and operated by trained professionals to ensure accuracy, reliability and compliance with project standards.

Software & Data Processing

We use industry-standard software for data processing, analysis and visualization, enabling accurate deliverables and faster project turnaround.

  • AutoCAD & Civil 3D
  • ArcGIS / QGIS
  • Drone Data Processing Software (DSM / DTM / Orthomosaic)
  • Contour & Volume Analysis Tools
  • GIS Database & Thematic Mapping Platforms

Project Execution & Cost Efficiency

At LandCad Services, we follow a structured project execution methodology that focuses on accuracy, time efficiency and cost optimization.

Our workflow minimizes rework, reduces on-site delays and ensures faster data availability, helping clients achieve better project control and reduced costs.
