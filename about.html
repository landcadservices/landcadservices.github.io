About LandCad

Who We Are

LandCad is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions firm based in Nagpur. We deliver accurate, technology-driven survey, GIS, drone mapping and infrastructure solutions for government and private sector projects.

Our team combines field expertise with modern tools such as Total Station, GNSS, UAVs (drones) and GIS platforms to ensure high-precision deliverables.

Our Vision

To become a trusted surveying and geospatial partner by delivering reliable data, innovative solutions and timely project execution.
