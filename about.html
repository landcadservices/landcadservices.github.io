About LandCad Services

Founded in February 2023 in Nagpur, Maharashtra, LandCad Services has grown into a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company.

We specialize in Land Surveying, Engineering Surveying and Aerial Surveying, delivering services to Government, Corporate and Private clients.

Safety, accuracy and quality are at the core of every project we undertake.

Vision & Mission

To become a trusted technology partner by delivering accurate, innovative and reliable surveying solutions.
