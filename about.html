About LandCad Services

Trusted Surveying & Geospatial Partner for Infrastructure Projects

Professional Surveying for Infrastructure & Development

LandCad Services is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company based in Nagpur, Maharashtra. We support government departments, infrastructure agencies, consultants and private organizations by delivering accurate, reliable and technology-driven survey data.

Our services play a critical role in planning, design, execution and monitoring of infrastructure projects including roads, railways, irrigation systems, mining operations, renewable energy projects and land development works.

Survey Instruments & Field Technology

LandCad Services deploys modern, calibrated and industry-approved instruments to ensure accuracy and consistency across all survey assignments.

  • Total Station – Engineering & Layout Surveys
  • D-GPS / GNSS Receivers – High-Accuracy Control Surveys
  • UAV / Drone Platforms – Aerial Mapping & Inspection
  • Auto Levels & Digital Levels
  • Advanced Field Data Collection Equipment

Software & Data Processing Capability

We process and analyze survey data using industry-standard software platforms to generate accurate, engineering-ready and decision-support outputs.

  • AutoCAD & Civil 3D
  • ArcGIS / QGIS
  • Drone Processing Software (Orthomosaic, DSM / DTM)
  • Contour, Volume & Area Computation Tools
  • GIS Databases & Thematic Mapping Systems
