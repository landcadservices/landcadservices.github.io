About LandCad Services

Professional Land Surveying & Geospatial Solutions

Who We Are

LandCad Services is a Nagpur-based professional land surveying and geospatial solutions firm established to support infrastructure, government and private development projects with accurate, dependable and regulation-compliant survey data.

We specialize in land surveying, engineering surveys, aerial mapping and GIS-based solutions, ensuring every project meets technical standards, statutory requirements and client expectations.

Our work philosophy is driven by precision, transparency, timely delivery and cost efficiency.

Our Project Approach

We follow a structured workflow that includes site reconnaissance, control establishment, detailed field data collection, digital processing, quality checks and final reporting.

This approach ensures accuracy, reduces rework, controls project costs and supports informed engineering and planning decisions.
