About LandCad Services

With a strong foundation in surveying principles and modern geospatial technologies, LandCad Services focuses on delivering data accuracy, operational efficiency and reliable outputs that support critical infrastructure and development decisions.

Our approach combines field expertise with advanced data processing, ensuring every survey meets project specifications, regulatory standards and client expectations.

LandCad Services is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company based in Nagpur, Maharashtra. We deliver accurate, reliable and technology-driven surveying services for infrastructure, agriculture, mining and development projects across India.

Our expertise covers land & boundary surveys, engineering and infrastructure surveys, drone-based aerial mapping and GIS solutions. We use modern instruments such as Total Stations, DGPS / GNSS systems and UAV platforms to ensure high-precision results.

At LandCad, our focus is on accuracy, safety and timely delivery. We work closely with government, corporate and private clients to support informed decision-making and successful project execution.

Vision & Mission

To become a trusted surveying and geospatial partner by delivering precise, innovative and dependable solutions while maintaining the highest standards of quality and professionalism.
