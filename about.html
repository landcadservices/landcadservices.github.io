Who We Are

Founded in February 2023 in Nagpur, Maharashtra, LandCad Services has grown into a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company serving Government, Corporate, and Private sector clients across India.

We specialize in Land Surveying, Engineering Surveying, and Aerial Surveying, delivering accurate, reliable, and technology-driven solutions for infrastructure, irrigation, mining, and development projects.
