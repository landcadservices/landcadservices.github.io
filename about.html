About LandCad Services

Founded in February 2023 and headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra, LandCad Services is a professional land surveying and geospatial solutions company delivering accurate, reliable and technology-driven services across India.

We specialize in Land Surveying, Engineering Surveying, Aerial & Drone Surveying, GIS Mapping and Advanced Geospatial Analysis. Our solutions support infrastructure development, irrigation projects, mining operations, renewable energy, transportation corridors and urban planning.

At LandCad, safety, accuracy and quality are at the core of every project. We use modern instruments such as Total Stations, DGPS/GNSS systems, UAV/Drone platforms and advanced GIS software to ensure precise data collection and dependable results.

Our team is committed to delivering projects on time while maintaining transparency, technical excellence and client satisfaction for Government, Corporate and Private sector clients.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a trusted technology partner by delivering accurate, innovative and reliable surveying and geospatial solutions.

Mission: To support infrastructure and development projects with high-quality survey data, advanced technology and professional expertise while maintaining the highest standards of safety and precision.
